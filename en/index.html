About Orpita Studio

Forging new realities, one story at a time.

Orpita is an indie game studio based in Egypt. Founded in May 2025, it started as a small team making games we enjoy ourselves. The studio experiments with ideas, builds worlds, and learns along the way. Each game reflects what excites us and what we love to create.
