About the game

One More Day is a short, emotional narrative experience set in the heart of a modern hospital.

Created by Orpita Game Studio for the GMTK Game Jam 2025 (Theme: “Loop”), the game blends story-driven exploration, tense medical decision-making, and human emotion into a single, impactful loop.

You play as Dr. Zain, a young doctor reliving the same critical day—each loop offering a chance to make different choices, face moral dilemmas, and discover the weight of every decision. From quiet hallways to life-or-death moments, One More Day explores the boundaries between duty, exhaustion, and compassion.

A heartfelt project built with love, collaboration, and creative passion from Orpita’s team and talented volunteers around the world.
